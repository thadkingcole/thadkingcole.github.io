Is It Open?

Do you need to get out of the house, but you don't know what is open and the current covid risk in your area? Is It Open will find your choice of restaurants, bars, and/or parks near you. Is It Open will also tell you up-to-date covid statistics in your area to help you properly assess the risk of going outside.

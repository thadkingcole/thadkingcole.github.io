About Me

My name is Thad, and I am an aspiring full-stack web developer.

I am originally from Tennessee and attended the University of Tennessee where I studied nuclear engineering. While in college, I was introduced to programming through various courses that taught MATLAB and Fortran, and I really enjoyed the thrill of solving a problem through code. Today, that thrill remains, and I am teaching myself Python and attending a coding bootcamp at the University of North Carolina to learn the skills necessary to become a full-stack web developer.
